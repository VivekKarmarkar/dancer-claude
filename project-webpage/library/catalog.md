## Patterns

**What works best:**
- Studio-shot dance videos with clean backgrounds
- Single dancer, front-facing
- Good lighting, minimal camera movement
- YouTube Shorts (short duration = fast extraction)

**What struggles:**
- Music videos with cuts, effects, multiple people
- Low lighting, busy backgrounds
- Rapid camera angle changes
